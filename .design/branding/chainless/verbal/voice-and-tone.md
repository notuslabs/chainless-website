# Voice & Tone

> Phase: verbal | Brand: chainless | Generated: 2026-03-22

---

## Voice Identity: Bold, Defiant, Warm

Chainless speaks like someone who's seen the old financial system from the inside, quietly built the replacement, and is now inviting you to join — with confidence, edge, and genuine care.

The voice is not a corporation talking. It's not a chatbot. It's not a suit. It's a knowledgeable friend who happens to have built something transformative — and who pushes back against the status quo with a warm smile, not a raised fist.

---

## The 60/40 Split

Chainless operates on a deliberate tension: **60% premium warmth / 40% defiant counter-positioning** in voice. (The visual system runs 70/30 — more premium. The voice is where the rebel energy lives.)

**Why the split?**
- The voice leads with edge; the design keeps the warmth. This is intentional.
- Pure premium warmth (100%) would make Chainless sound like Nubank. Friendly, but indistinguishable.
- Pure defiance (100%) would make Chainless sound like a crypto rebel. Edgy, but untrustworthy for wealth.
- The 60/40 blend creates a voice that feels confident enough to challenge the status quo AND warm enough to manage your life savings.

**How it works in practice:**
- The 60% warmth: empathetic, clear, generous, respectful of intelligence
- The 40% defiance: provocative truths, counter-positioning, naming what others won't, quiet confidence that the old way is over
- The defiance never turns to aggression. The warmth never turns to blandness.

| Context | Warmth | Defiance | Example |
|---------|:------:|:--------:|---------|
| Homepage hero | 50% | 50% | "O sistema financeiro foi construido para eles. Chainless foi construido para voce." |
| Onboarding | 80% | 20% | "Seus ativos, suas chaves. Vamos configurar sua soberania." |
| Marketing campaign | 40% | 60% | "Sem custodiantes. Sem intermediarios. Sem pedir permissao." |
| Error state | 90% | 10% | "Algo deu errado — mas seus ativos estao seguros na sua carteira." |
| Social media | 50% | 50% | "Todo mundo quer cuidar do seu dinheiro. A gente construiu um jeito de voce cuidar sozinho." |
| Legal / compliance | 95% | 5% | Clear, warm, regulatory-compliant language |
| Security page | 60% | 40% | "Autocustodia nao e uma feature. E a arquitetura inteira." |

## Voice Attributes

### 1. Confident

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Means** | Speaks with the assurance of someone who built the architecture. Doesn't hedge, doesn't apologize for being different, doesn't seek permission. States truths directly. |
| **Doesn't mean** | Arrogant, dismissive, or condescending. Never talks down to users. Never implies competitors' users are foolish. |
| **Do** | "Seus ativos nunca saem da sua carteira." / "Your assets never leave your wallet." |
| **Don't** | "Acreditamos que possivelmente seus ativos podem estar mais seguros conosco." / "We believe your assets might possibly be safer with us." |
| **Do** | "Autocustodia e a unica arquitetura seria para patrimonio digital." |
| **Don't** | "Algumas pessoas acham que autocustodia pode ser uma boa opcao." |

### 2. Defiant

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Means** | Names uncomfortable truths the industry avoids. Questions what everyone accepts. Positions Chainless as the answer to a structural problem, not just a product alternative. The defiance comes from having already built the solution — it's confident, not angry. |
| **Doesn't mean** | Aggressive, preachy, or combative. Never attacks people — targets systems. Never crypto-bro "WAGMI" energy. Never regulatory-triggering ("banco"). |
| **Do** | "Banks hold your money because they can. We built a world where they can't." |
| **Don't** | "Banks are evil and stealing from you!! Wake up!!" |
| **Do** | "O sistema financeiro foi construido para eles. Chainless foi construido para voce." |
| **Don't** | "Pare de ser trouxa e saia do banco agora." |

### 3. Warm

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Means** | Genuinely cares about the user. Explains without condescending. Makes complex things feel approachable. Celebrates the user's intelligence. Feels like a conversation, not a broadcast. Has Brazilian cultural warmth without being folksy. |
| **Doesn't mean** | Saccharine, performatively friendly, or emotionally manipulative. Never "Hey bestie!" energy. Never uses warmth to avoid hard truths. |
| **Do** | "Rendimento de 8.2% este mes. Tudo na sua carteira, como sempre." / "8.2% yield this month. In your wallet, as always." |
| **Don't** | "OMG congrats on your amazing yield!! 🎉🚀" |
| **Do** | "Precisa de ajuda? Estamos aqui." |
| **Don't** | "Submeta um ticket e aguarde atendimento." |

### 4. Clear

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Means** | Says what it means on the first read. No jargon without context. No weasel words. No corporate fluff. Respects the reader's time. Uses concrete language — numbers, specifics, facts — not abstractions. |
| **Doesn't mean** | Dumbed down or patronizing. Can use technical terms when the audience knows them (Marina). Simplicity != shallowness. |
| **Do** | "Seu patrimonio rendeu R$4.200 este mes via estrategias DeFi — tudo na sua carteira." |
| **Don't** | "Otimizamos seus retornos atraves da alavancagem sinergica de protocolos descentralizados de liquidez." |
| **Do** | "Self-custody means you hold the keys. No one else can access your assets." |
| **Don't** | "We leverage decentralized custodial architecture paradigms to optimize asset sovereignty vectors." |

## Style Rules

### Grammar & Mechanics

| Rule | Chainless Standard |
|------|-------------------|
| **Contractions** | Yes in PT-BR (natural speech). Yes in EN for casual/marketing contexts. No in legal/formal. |
| **Oxford comma** | Yes (EN). Follow standard rules (PT-BR). |
| **Exclamation marks** | Sparingly — max 1 per page. Never in headlines. Acceptable in success microcopy ("Pronto!"). |
| **Emoji** | Social media only, sparingly. Never in product UI. Never in headlines. |
| **Ellipsis** | Avoid — feels uncertain. Chainless is confident. |
| **First person** | "Nos" / "We" for brand voice. "A Chainless" in formal/legal contexts. |
| **Addressing user** | "Voce" / "You" — always direct, never formal "o senhor/a senhora." |
| **Sentence length** | Average 15-20 words. Headlines: 5-10 words. Body: mix short punchy with medium explanation. |
| **Paragraphs** | Max 3-4 sentences. Shorter in UI. One idea per paragraph. |
| **Numbers** | Use digits for amounts and data (R$4.200, 8.2%, 180+ ativos). Spell out small counts in editorial (tres pilares). |

### Language Hierarchy

| Context | Language |
|---------|----------|
| Primary market (Brazil) | PT-BR always. Natural, modern Brazilian Portuguese — not formal/literary. |
| International / technical | EN. Clear, direct. American English spelling. |
| Mixed audience (social, global campaigns) | PT-BR primary with EN subtitles/translations. |
| Product UI | PT-BR default, EN available. Localized, not translated. |
| Internal / technical docs | EN acceptable. |

### Words We Use / Words We Don't

| Use | Instead Of | Why |
|-----|-----------|-----|
| Patrimonio digital | Cripto / criptomoedas | Premium framing over commodity framing |
| Autocustodia | Carteira descentralizada | Accessible, clear, ownable |
| Plataforma de patrimonio | Carteira / exchange / app | Category definition |
| Estrategias de rendimento | Farming / staking / pools | Wealth language, not DeFi jargon |
| Chaves privadas | Seeds / mnemonics | When explaining to non-technical users |
| Soberania | Descentralizacao | Emotional, human — not technical, abstract |
| Patrimonio | Investimento | Wealth > investment (broader, premium) |
| Rendimento | Yield / APY | PT-BR first; use yield/APY only in technical contexts |

### Naming Conventions

| Type | Convention | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Brand | Always "Chainless" — never abbreviated, never lowercase in running text | "Chainless" not "chainless" or "CL" |
| Brand platform | "Torne-se Chainless" — always with period when standalone | "Torne-se Chainless." |
| Product features | Descriptive PT-BR names, not branded sub-products | "Estrategias de rendimento" not "Chainless Yield" |
| Tiers / plans | If needed: use sovereignty language, not banking language | "Soberano" not "Premium" or "Gold" |
| Content series | "Torne-se Chainless: [topic]" framework | "Torne-se Chainless: Entenda autocustodia" |
| Internal categories | Can use "banco onchain" shorthand — NEVER customer-facing | Internal docs only |

### Punctuation: No Em Dashes

Never use em dashes ( — ) in any customer-facing text. Replace with periods, commas, or restructure the sentence. Em dashes feel literary and interrupt the reading flow. Chainless copy should feel direct and clean.

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| "Seus ativos são seus — na blockchain" | "Seus ativos são seus. Na blockchain, sob sua chave." |
| "Sem seed phrases — sem custódia" | "Sem seed phrases, sem custódia." |
| "Privacidade e facilidade — a Chainless entrega" | "Privacidade e facilidade. A Chainless entrega..." |

Page titles use `|` as separator: "Chainless \| Patrimonio Digital Soberano".

### Words We Never Use (Customer-Facing)

| Forbidden | Why | Alternative |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Banco / bank | Brazilian regulatory risk | "Plataforma de patrimonio digital" |
| WAGMI / NGMI / GM | Crypto-bro culture — antithetical to brand | Just don't |
| "Confie em nos" / "Trust us" | Contradicts self-custody thesis — we don't ask for trust | "Verifique voce mesmo" / "Don't trust — verify" |
| "Facil" / "Easy" | Commodity language — premium brands don't say easy | "Simples" / "Intuitivo" / "Acessivel" |
| "Melhor" / "Best" | Unsubstantiated superlatives damage credibility | Be specific about what's different |
| "Revolucionario" / "Revolutionary" | Hyperbolic, empty | Show don't tell — the architecture speaks |
