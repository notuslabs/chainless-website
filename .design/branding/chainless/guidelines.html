Motion

Reveal, Don't Announce

The Magician's entrance — elements emerge from depth, not slam into view. Blur-fade-up is the signature. Springs for interaction, easing for presentation.

Premium Easing
cubic-bezier(0.32, 0.72, 0, 1)
Confident arrival. Moderate acceleration, slow deceleration to rest. No bounce, no overshoot.
Spring Snappy
stiffness: 200, damping: 20
Quick response for buttons, magnetic interactions, tap feedback.
Spring Smooth
stiffness: 120, damping: 24
Gentle organic motion for number reveals, scale-in effects.
Blur-Fade-Up (Signature)
opacity 0→1, y 40→0, blur 10→0, 0.9s
The "materializing" entrance that maps to the Magician archetype. Scroll-triggered, once.
Stagger
120ms between children
Sequential reveal of related elements. 60ms for word-by-word text.
Magnetic Button
15% offset, SPRING_SNAPPY, tap 0.97
Cursor-tracking hover with spring physics. Subtle pull toward cursor position.

Voice & Tone

Bold. Defiant. Warm.

The voice is 60% premium, 40% rebel. We speak with confidence about sovereignty. We never plead, never hedge, never use commodity crypto language.

Bold
Means
  • Declarative statements, not suggestions
  • Strong verbs: "Build", "Own", "Transform"
  • Short, confident sentences
  • Direct address: "You" not "users"
Doesn't Mean
  • Aggressive or confrontational
  • ALL CAPS SHOUTING
  • Superlatives without substance
  • Hype language or urgency manipulation
Defiant
Means
  • Question the status quo of custody
  • Challenge intermediaries, not people
  • "Why should someone else hold your keys?"
  • Sovereignty as an architectural fact
Doesn't Mean
  • Anti-government or conspiratorial
  • Fear-mongering about banks
  • Crypto-anarchist posturing
  • Attacking competitors by name
Warm
Means
  • Inviting tone: "Torne-se Chainless"
  • Acknowledge complexity, simplify it
  • Human language, not technical jargon
  • Brazilian warmth in Portuguese copy
Doesn't Mean
  • Casual or overly friendly
  • Emoji-heavy communication
  • Patronizing explanations
  • Softening sovereignty claims

Design Principles

Five rules that break ties

1
Warm Authority
Premium without coldness. Confident without arrogance.
Every surface carries warm undertones. Blue-gray is institutional finance. Chainless is warm amber — the color of gold, of Brazilian sunlight. #1C1B19 not #000000.
2
Restraint as Luxury
The 10% accent rule. Whitespace is breathing room.
Brand yellow appears at 10% of any composition. The remaining 90% is warm neutrals and dark surfaces. Every element on screen must justify its presence.
3
Reveal, Don't Announce
The Magician's entrance — elements emerge from depth.
The blur-fade-up signature creates a "materializing" feel. Animations trigger once on scroll entry and stay. No bounce, no elastic, no attention-grabbing pulses.
4
Architecture is the Brand
Self-custody is structural truth, not a feature checkbox.
Show the structure. Let the Doppelrand's double border reveal its construction. Let data values appear in monospace. No decorative dishonesty.
5
Dual Voice, One System
The Magician writes the headlines. The Ruler builds the interface.
Fraunces speaks at brand moments. Satoshi governs product. IBM Plex Mono presents data. The 70/30 archetype ratio manifests at the typographic level.

Anti-Patterns

Never do this

× Use pure #000000 or black — always warm dark
× Use Tailwind default shadows — they break warm tinting
× Use Fraunces for body text, nav, buttons, or forms
× Use yellow as a section background fill
× Use more than 3 dark/inverted sections per page
× Use bounce, elastic, or spring-overshoot easing
× Auto-play animations that can't be paused
× Use cool-tinted grays (blue undertone)
× Use "banco" in customer-facing content
× Target people — target systems
