## Key Findings

### White Space Identified
The upper-right quadrant (Premium + Self-Custody) has **zero competitors globally**. Three structural barriers prevent entry:
- Custodial players cannot abandon their custody business model
- Self-custody players lack premium brand infrastructure
- Traditional banks cannot pivot messaging from "trust us" to "don't trust anyone"

### Category Creation
"Sovereign digital wealth" — a new category where self-custody is the defining characteristic, not a feature checkbox. Customer-facing label: "Plataforma de patrimonio digital com autocustodia."

### Demand Signals
- Self-custody wallet users growing at ~55% CAGR
- Post-FTX trust recalibration among sophisticated investors
- Brazilian HNW crypto allocation rising (5-15% of investable assets) with no self-custody wealth option
- 18-24 month window to establish category ownership
