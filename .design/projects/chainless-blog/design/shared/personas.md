# Personas
> Phase: design | Project: chainless-blog | Generated: 2026-04-08

---

## Primary Persona: Rafael, 32

**Demographics:** Software engineer in Sao Paulo, earns R$15-25k/month. Holds crypto since 2021. Reads tech blogs in English but prefers Portuguese for financial content. Uses iPhone 15 Pro and MacBook.

**Goals:**
- Understand DeFi yields vs. CDI in his native language
- Learn self-custody without relying on YouTube gurus
- Find a trusted platform that aligns with his values (sovereignty, transparency)

**Pain points:**
- Most Portuguese crypto content is shallow clickbait or shill
- Doesn't trust influencer-driven platforms
- Wants data-backed analysis, not hype

**Usage context:** Reads articles on mobile during commute (40%), desktop at home in the evening (60%). Scans TL;DR first, reads full article if the thesis is compelling. Bookmarks articles to revisit. Shares on Twitter/LinkedIn.

**Reading behavior:** Skims headings first, reads in 10-15 minute sessions. Expects code blocks to be syntax-highlighted. Compares yield tables carefully. Uses TOC to jump to specific sections.

---

## Secondary Persona: Camila, 27

**Demographics:** Financial analyst in Curitiba, earns R$8-12k/month. New to crypto (< 1 year). Comfortable with traditional finance terminology. Uses Android phone and Windows laptop.

**Goals:**
- Understand crypto basics without the jargon wall
- Compare crypto products to traditional investments she already knows
- Feel confident before making her first non-exchange purchase

**Pain points:**
- Crypto content assumes too much prior knowledge
- Overwhelmed by the number of protocols and options
- Wants a clear "start here" path

**Usage context:** Discovers articles via Google search (Portuguese queries like "rendimento cripto vs CDI"). Reads primarily on mobile. Needs the TL;DR to decide if the article is worth her time. Unlikely to browse the listing page — arrives directly at articles via search.

---

## Edge Persona: Pedro, 45

**Demographics:** Small business owner in Belo Horizonte. Not tech-savvy. Heard about Bitcoin from his accountant. Uses an older Android phone with larger text settings.

**Goals:**
- Understand what self-custody means in plain language
- Know if Chainless is safe for someone who isn't technical

**Pain points:**
- Small text is hard to read
- Complex layouts confuse him
- Needs the simplest possible navigation

**Usage context:** Arrives from WhatsApp shared links. Reads on mobile only. Needs the article to work perfectly at 200% zoom with increased text spacing. Will not use TOC — scrolls linearly.
