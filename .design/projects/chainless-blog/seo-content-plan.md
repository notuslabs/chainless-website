# Chainless Blog — SEO Content Plan

## Strategic Framework

**Goal:** Capture organic search traffic from financially sophisticated Brazilians exploring DeFi, self-custody, and digital wealth — then convert them into Chainless users.

**Content pillars** mapped to the product's three value props:
1. **Soberania Digital** (Sovereignty) — self-custody education, post-FTX trust crisis
2. **Crescimento Patrimonial** (Wealth Growth) — DeFi yields, comparisons with CDI/SELIC/poupança
3. **Vida Financeira Prática** (Practical Finance) — card, Pix, day-to-day crypto usage

**Language:** Portuguese (BR), with selected pillar pieces in English for future international SEO.

## SEO Architecture Recommendations

### Internal linking strategy
- Every top-of-funnel article links to a mid-funnel comparison piece
- Every comparison piece links to a branded/bottom-funnel article
- All articles link to the relevant product page (yield, card, lending)

### Content structure per article
- 1,500–2,500 words (sweet spot for Portuguese crypto SEO)
- H1 = exact target keyword, H2s = related long-tails
- TL;DR box at the top for featured snippet capture
- "Como a Chainless resolve isso" section near the bottom (soft CTA)
- Schema markup: FAQPage + Article
