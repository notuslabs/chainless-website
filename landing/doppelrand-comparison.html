5. Radius Comparison

The most visible change. 36px to 18px shifts the entire silhouette language from "consumer tech" to "architectural precision."

36px radius (current)
Pillowy, rounded. Reads as consumer tech — AirPods case, iOS widgets, modern fintech apps.
18px radius (proposed)
Rectilinear, precise. Reads as architectural — Cartier packaging, leather portfolios, Swiss watch boxes.

Summary of Changes

  • Border radius 36px to 18px — shifts silhouette from consumer tech to architectural luxury (Cartier, not AirPods)
  • Glow orbs removed entirely — wealth absorbs light, it doesn't project it
  • Outer shell opacity 2% to 3% — less glassmorphism, more solid material presence
  • Inner gradient simplified — 2-stop at 160deg instead of 3-stop at 145deg, quieter and more confident
  • Gold hallmark accent added — 1px gradient gold line at inner top edge, 24px inset, fades at edges
  • Hover redesigned — subtle 1px vertical lift instead of ring brightening + shadow burst
  • Double-shell preserved — the "box within a box" metaphor stays, reframed as picture-frame matting
  • 6px gap preserved — the matting stays exactly as-is
  • Inner highlight preserved — top hairline catching light, like sheen on dark leather
